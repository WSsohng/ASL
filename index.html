Analytical Spectroscopy
for
Chemical Analysis

ASL develops quantitative and interpretable analytical workflows for complex chemical systems. We combine spectroscopy, signal preprocessing, and chemometrics to solve translational problems in bio, materials, and environmental applications.

Publication Archive Gallery Archive

Laboratory Scope

Measurement, Modeling, Validation.

We execute end-to-end analytical pipelines from instrument setup and spectral acquisition to model validation. Our objective is robust, reproducible quantitation under real-world measurement variability.

Research Focus

Research Themes

Biological & Clinical Samples

01 / 05

생체유래 시료(소변, 담즙, 체액 등)에서 질병 관련 신호를 정량화하고, 전처리부터 검증까지 임상 적용 가능성을 고려한 분석 프로토콜을 설계합니다.

We establish spectroscopy-based quantitative workflows for clinical matrices, integrating Raman/SERS and NIR fingerprints with robust validation to support translational diagnostics.

Topics: Clinical diagnostics, urine/bile analysis, biomarker quantitation

Food, Agriculture & Environment

02 / 05

식품 원산지, 품질, 위변조 판별과 환경 시료 모니터링을 위해 비파괴 분광분석 기반의 분류·정량 모델을 개발합니다.

We combine molecular and elemental spectral signatures for origin discrimination, quality assessment, and contamination screening in complex food and environmental matrices.

Topics: Origin authentication, quality control, contamination screening

Spectroscopic Platforms

03 / 05

Raman, SERS, NIR, 전기화학-분광 융합 등 복수의 플랫폼을 시료 특성에 맞게 선택·결합하여 감도, 선택성, 재현성을 높입니다.

We engineer multimodal spectroscopy systems, including transmission Raman, reusable SERS substrates, and electrochemical-coupled sensing, to maximize sensitivity and reproducibility.

Topics: Raman/SERS/NIR, multimodal sensing, substrate engineering

Chemometrics & AI Modeling

04 / 05

PCA, PLS, PLS-DA, SVM, 딥러닝 기반 모델을 사용해 스펙트럼 데이터를 해석하고, 과적합을 줄인 재현 가능한 정량·분류 모델을 구축합니다.

Our chemometric pipeline integrates preprocessing, variable selection, and multivariate or machine-learning models (PCA, PLS, PLS-DA, SVM, deep learning) for robust prediction.

Topics: Data fusion, model validation, explainable prediction

Quantitative Translation

05 / 05

연구실 수준의 분석법을 실제 사용 환경으로 확장하기 위해 교정 이전(calibration transfer), 장비 간 편차 보정, 반복 재현성 검증까지 포함한 전주기 평가를 수행합니다.

We emphasize deployable quantitative analytics through calibration transfer, inter-instrument harmonization, and long-term robustness tests under practical measurement conditions.

Topics: Calibration transfer, robustness, real-world deployment

Professor | Analytical Chemistry | Hanyang University

  • Industrial R&D at SK Energy: NIR online process monitoring systems
